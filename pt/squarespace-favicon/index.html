Add a Favicon to Squarespace

Upgrade your site aesthetics. Learn how to configure a clean tab favicon inside Squarespace settings.

Squarespace Favicon Setup Instructions

  1. Log in to your Squarespace account and edit your target website.
  2. In the Home Menu, click on Settings, then click Logo & Title (or **Design > Browser Icon** in newer versions).
  3. Scroll down to the Browser Icon (Favicon) section.
  4. Click the upload button or drag and drop your PNG favicon file.
  5. Click Save at the top left. Reload your page in a fresh browser to check.
